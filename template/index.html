Face Mask Detector

This web application uses deep learning combined with computer vision to detect people wearing a face mask correctly , incorrectly or not through live streams videos, uploaded URL and pictures.


EXPLORE





What Does The System Do?

With the growing need for safety and health monitoring, face mask detection is crucial. The most efficient way to ensure compliance with mask-wearing regulations is through automated detection systems. This application uses advanced deep learning to predict whether someone is wearing a face mask correctly, incorrectly, or not at all. Our system provides real-time monitoring with high accuracy.

Our Services

Upload Picture

This site detects if people in the uploaded picture are wearing a face mask correctly, incorrectly or not. The number of faces detected and the mask status will be displayed.



Take Image from Webcam

This site detects if people in the live-video stream are wearing a face mask correctly, incorrectly or not. Real-time detection with instant feedback on mask compliance.

Insert Image URL

This site detects if people in the image from a URL are wearing a face mask correctly, incorrectly or not. Simply provide the image URL and get instant analysis.

Use Cases


Hospitals

Using Face Mask Detection System, Hospitals can monitor if their staff is wearing masks during their shift or not. If any health worker is found without a mask, they will receive a notification with a reminder to wear a mask. Also, if quarantine people who are required to wear a mask, the system can keep an eye and detect if the mask is present or not and send notification automatically or report to the authorities.


Airports

The Face Mask Detection System can be used at airports to detect travelers without masks. Face data can be captured at the entrance. If a traveler is found without a face mask, their picture is sent to airport authorities for quick action.


Offices

The Face Mask Detection System can be used at office premises to detect if employees are maintaining safety standards at work. It monitors employees without masks and sends them a reminder to wear a mask. The reports can be downloaded or sent an email at the end of the day to capture people who are not complying with the regulations or the requirements.
